Single Restaurant Orders
Users can order food from a specific restaurant of their choice. Swiggy picks up the order from that restaurant and delivers it directly to the customer's location.
Feature Details
Delivery Method Direct from the restaurant
Restaurant Selection Choose from a variety of restaurants
Delivery Time Estimated delivery time displayed
Cloud Kitchen Orders
Swiggy also partners with cloud kitchens or virtual restaurants that operate solely for delivery. These kitchens may not have a physical dine-in presence and prepare food exclusively for online orders placed through platforms like Swiggy.
Feature Details
Delivery Method Delivered from cloud kitchens
Kitchen Partnerships Exclusive kitchens for online orders
Food Variety Wide range of cuisines available
